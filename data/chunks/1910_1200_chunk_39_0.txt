Figure A.7.1( b )—Hazard Category for Effects on or Via Lactation ----------------------------------------------------------------- EFFECTS ON OR VIA LACTATION Effects on or via lactation shall be classified in a separate single category. Chemicals that are absorbed by women and have been shown to interfere with lactation or that may be present (including metabolites) in breast milk in amounts sufficient to cause concern for the health of a breastfed child, shall be classified to indicate this property hazardous to breastfed babies.
